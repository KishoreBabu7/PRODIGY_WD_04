About Me

My name is Tulugu Kishore Babu, and I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) degree in Computer Science Engineering at Centurion University of Technology and Management. My educational journey began at St. Joseph's English Medium School in Paralakhemundi, Odisha, where I completed my matriculation. I furthered my studies at Akshara Senior Secondary School in Pathapatnam, Andhra Pradesh, where I completed my intermediate education. In addition to my academic pursuits, I am actively involved as an NCC (National Cadet Corps) cadet at Centurion University. This experience has not only enriched my personal growth but has also instilled in me a sense of discipline and leadership. At 18 years old, I am driven by a passion for technology and a desire to make meaningful contributions in the field of computer science. I come from a close-knit family and have one sibling. My upbringing has taught me the values of hard work, determination, and perseverance, which I apply to both my academic and personal endeavors. As I continue my journey in computer science, I am excited about the opportunities to learn, innovate, and contribute to the ever-evolving technological landscape.
